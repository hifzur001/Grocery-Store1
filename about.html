about us

Naturix Farm & Food Care ney york is a registered new york based and whole-sector coalition made up of representatives from all farming types and associated businesses and positions itself as the helpful expert on new york agriculture. Farm & Food Care new york brings farmers, agricultural professionals, related businesses, government organizations and other groups together with a mandate to build public trust in food and farming in new york and across ney york. Our sister organizations, Farm & Food Care Saskatchewan and Farm & Food Care P.E.I, follow a similar mandate in each respective province. Both organizations collaborate on national initiatives

our vision

earn public trust in new york

we go beyond organic

Our regenerative farming methods aim to produce the healthiest fruit and vegetables nature can grow. At the same time, we foster living soil and functioning ecosystems that work to retain water and provide plants with the nutrients they need

our location & work

Welcome to our farms. Our farms are geographically spread over seven countries and three continents. We benefit from a wide range of soil types and climates to grow some of the most amazing fruit and vegetables. All our farms are currently implementing a regenerative agriculture strategy. This approach increases the quality of fruit and vegetables and has a positive impact on both the agroecosystem and the natural ecosystem.

Our six farms benefit from a diverse range of soil types and climates (deciduous and subtropical). We supply a wide variety of fruit throughout the year such as apples, pears, avocados and grapes.
